CHAPTER 6

And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, 2‘Speak unto the children of Israel; and thou shalt say unto them, “Man or woman, whosoever shall specially vow a vow to separate himself in purity unto the LORD; 3he shall separate himself with purity from wine and strong drink; and he shall be pure from wine, and vinegar of wine, and vinegar of strong drink; and whatsoever is made of the grape shall he not drink, neither eat fresh grapes or raisins. 4All the days of his vow he shall he eat none of all the things that come from the vine, wine from the grape-stones even to the husk. 5All the days of the vow of his separation there shall no razor come upon his head, until the days be fulfilled, in the which he vowed unto the LORD. He shall be holy, cherishing let the locks of the hair of his head grow 6all the days of his vow unto the LORD. He shall not come nigh to any soul that hath departed; 7unto his father, or to his mother, or to his brother or his sister.
