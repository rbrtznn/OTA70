CHAPTER 1

These be the words which Moses spake unto all Israel on the far side Jordan in the wilderness, toward the west nigh unto the Red Sea, between Paran, and Tophel, and Laban, and Olon, and the gold works. 2It is an eleven days’ journey from Horeb by the way of mount Seir unto Kadesh-Barnea. 3And it came to pass in the fortieth year, in the eleventh month, on the first day of the month, that Moses spake unto all the children of Israel, according unto all things that the LORD had given him in commandment unto them. 4After he had smitten Sihon the king of the Amorites, which dwelt in Heshbon, and Og the king of Bashan, which dwelt in Ashtaroth and in Edrai, 5on the far side Jordan, in the land of Moab, began Moses to declare this law, saying, 6‘The LORD our God spake unto us in Horeb, saying, “Ye have dwelt long enough in this mount. 7Turn ye and depart, and go to the mount of the Amorites, and unto all them which dwell round about Arabah, unto the mount and the plain, and unto the south, and the land of the Canaanites nigh unto the sea and Antilebanon, unto the great river Euphrates. 8Behold, I have delivered the land before you; go in, inherit the land which I sware unto your fathers, to Abraham, and Isaac, and Jacob, to give unto them and to their seed after them.” 9And I spake unto you at that time, saying, “I shall not be able to bear you myself alone. 10The LORD your God hath multiplied you, and behold, ye are this day as the stars of heaven for multitude. 11The LORD God of your fathers increase you a thousand times so many more as ye are, and bless you, as He hath spoken unto you! 12How can I myself alone be able to bear your travail, and your cumbrance, and your strifes? 13Give you wise men, and understanding, and discerning among your tribes, and I will make them rulers over you.” 14And ye answered me and said, “The thing which thou hast spoken is good for us to do”. 15And I took of you men wise, and understanding, and discerning, and made them to be heads over you - captains of thousands, and captains of hundreds, and captains of fifties, and captains of tens, and scribes unto your judges. 16And I charged your judges at that time, saying, “Hear the causes between your brethren, and judge righteously between a man and his brother, and the stranger that is with him. 17Thou shalt not have respect unto a person in judgment; thou shalt judge according to small and great alike; thou shalt not shrink before the face of man, for the judgment is God’s; and whatsoever cause shall be too hard for you, ye shall bring it unto me, and I will hear it.” 18And I charged upon you at that time all the things which ye should do. 19And we departed from Horeb, and went through all that great wilderness and terrible, which ye saw, by the way of the mountain of the Amorite, as the LORD our God commanded us; and we came to Kadesh-Barnea. 20And I said unto you, “Ye are come unto the mountain of the Amorite, which the LORD our God doth give unto you. 21Behold, the LORD your God hath given up unto you the land before you. Go up and inherit it, as the LORD God of your fathers hath said unto you. “Fear not, neither be afraid.” 22And ye came unto me, every one of you, and said, “Let us send men before us, and let them search us the land, and bring us word again by what way we shall go up therein, and of the cities wherein we shall come”. 23And the thing pleased me, and I took twelve men of you, one of a tribe. 24And they turned, and went up into the mountain, and came unto the Valley of the Cluster, and searched it out. 25And they took of the fruit of the land in their hands, and brought it down unto you, and said, “It is a good land which the LORD our God doth give us.” 26 27 And ye would not go up, but rebelled against the word of the LORD our God;and ye murmured in your tents and said, “Because the LORD hated us, He hath brought us forth out of the land of Egypt, to deliver us into the hands of the Amorites to destroy us. 28Whither shall we go up?” And your brethren turned away your heart, saying, “The nation is great and of many people and mightier than we; the cities also are great and walled up to heaven, and moreover we have seen the sons of the giants there.” 29Then I said unto you, “Dread not, neither be afraid of them. 30The 168
