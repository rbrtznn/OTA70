52 53 And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, ‘Unto these the land shall be divided, that they may inherit according to the number of their names. 54To the greater number thou shalt give the greater inheritance, and to the lesser number thou shalt give the lesser inheritance; to each one as they have been numbered shall their inheritance be given. 55The land shall be divided unto the names by lot; according to the tribes of their divisions they shall inherit. 56By lot shalt thou divide the inheritance thereof between the many and the few.’

57And the sons of Levi after their divisions; to Gershon, the division of the Gershonites; to Kohath, the division of the Kohathites; to Merari, the division of the Merarites. 58These are the divisions of the sons of Levi; the division of the Libnites, the division of the Hebronites, the division of the Korahites, and the division of the Mushites; and Kohath begat Amram. 59And the name of his wife was Jochebed the daughter of Levi, who bare these to Levi in Egypt, and she bare unto Amram, Aaron, and Moses, and Miriam their sister. 60And unto Aaron was born Nadab and Abiud and Eleazar and Ithamar. 61And Nadab and Abiud died when they offered strange fire before the LORD in the wilderness of Sinai. 62And there were according to their numbering twenty and three thousand, every male from a month old and upward; for they were not numbered among the children of Israel because they have no inheritance among the children of Israel. 63And this is the numbering of Moses and Eleazar the priest, who numbered the children of Israel in Araboth of Moab, at Jordan by Jericho. 64And among these there was not a man numbered by Moses and Aaron 156
