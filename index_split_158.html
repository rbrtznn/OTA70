16And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, ‘Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, 17“Be ye at enmity with the Midianites, and smite them; 18for they hate you with the deceitfulness wherewith they they have beguiled in the matter of Peor, and in the matter of Cozbi, the daughter of a prince of Midian, their sister, which was slain in the day of the plague for Peor’s sake.” ’

CHAPTER 26

And it came to pass after the plague, that the LORD spake unto Moses and unto Eleazar the priest, saying, 2‘Take the sum of all the congregation of the children of Israel, from twenty years old and upward, according to the houses of their fathers’ families, every one that goeth forth to do battle in Israel’. 3And Moses and Eleazar the priest spake in Araboth of Moab by Jordan over against Jericho, saying, 4‘From twenty years old and upward’, as the LORD commanded Moses. And the children of Israel, which came forth out of Egypt, were; 5Reuben, the firstborn of Israel, and the sons of Reuben; unto Enoch, the division of Enoch; unto Pallu, the division of the Palluites. 6Unto Hezron, the division of the Hezronites; unto Carmi, the division of the Carmites. 7These are the divisions of Reuben, and their numbering was forty and three thousand and seven hundred and thirty. 8And the sons of Phallu; Eliab. 9And the sons of Eliab; Nemuel and Dathan and Abiram; these are the men summoned of the congregation, these are they that rose up against Moses and against Aaron in the gathering of Korah, in the rebellion against the LORD. 10And the earth opened her mouth, and swallowed them up and Korah also, when their company died, what time the fire devoured the two hundred and fifty; and these were made a sign. 11 12 Nothwithstanding the sons of Korah died not. And the sons of Simeon; the division of the sons of Simeon. To Nemuel, the division of the Nemuelites; to Jamin, the division of the Jaminites; to Jachin, the division of the Jachinites. 13To Zerah, the division of the Zerahites; to Shaul, the division of the Shaulites. 14These are the division of Simeon, according to their numbering, twenty and two thousand and two hundred. 15And the sons of Judah; Er and Onan; and Er and Onan died in the land of Canaan. 16And these were the sons of Judah, according to their division; to Shelah, the division of the Shelahites; to Perez, the division of the Perezites; to Zerah, the division of the Zerahites. 17And the sons of Perez were; to Hezron, the division of the Hezronites; to Hamul, the division of the Hamulites. 18These are the people of Judah, according to their numbering, threescore and sixteen thousand and five hundred. 19And the sons of Issachar according to their divisions; to Tola, the division of the Tolaites; to Puvah, the division of the Puvahites. 20To Jashub, the division of the Jashubites; to Shimron, the division of the Shimronites. 21These are the divisions of Issachar, according to their numbering, threescore and four thousand and three hundred. 22The sons of Zebulun, according to their divisions; to Sered, the division of the Seredites; to Elon, the division of the Elonites; to Jahleel, the division of the Jahleelites. 23These are the divisions of Zebulun, according to their numbering, threescore thousand and five hundred. 24The sons of Gad, according to their divisions; to Zephon, the division of the Zephonites; 25to Haggi, the division of the Haggites; to Azeni, the division of the Azenites; to Addi the division of the Addites; 26 27 to Arodi, the division of the Arodites; to Areli, the division of the Arelites.
