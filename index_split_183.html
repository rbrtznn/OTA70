The eyes of the LORD thy God are upon it from the beginning of the year even unto the end of the year.

13Now if ye will indeed hearken unto all the commandments which I command you this day, to love the LORD thy God, and to serve Him with all thine heart, and with all thy soul; 14then He will give unto thy land the early and the latter rain in its due season, and thou shalt gather in thy corn, and thy wine, and thine oil. 15And He shall give fodder in thy fields for thy cattle. 16And when thou hast eaten, and art filled, take heed to thyself that thine heart be not made fat, and ye turn aside, and serve other gods, and worship them; 17and the LORD be wroth with you, and He shut up the heavens, and there be no rain, and the land yield no fruit; and lest ye be destroyed quickly from off the good land which the LORD hath given you. 18And ye shall lay up these words in your heart and in your soul, and ye shall bind them for a sign upon your hand, and they shall be immovable before your eyes. 19And ye shall teach them your children, to speak of them when thou sittest in thine house and when thou walkest by the way; when thou liest down and when thou risest up. 20And ye shall write them upon the door-posts of your houses, and upon your gates, 21that your days may be long, and the days of your children, in the land which the LORD sware unto your fathers to give them, as the days of heaven upon the earth. 22And it shall come to pass that if ye will indeed hearken unto all these commandments which I command you this day, to observe them, to love the LORD our God, and to walk in all His ways, and to cleave close unto Him; 23then will the LORD drive out all these nations from before you, and ye shall inherit great nations and mightier than yourselves. 24Every place whereon the sole of your foot shall tread shall be yours, from the wilderness and Antilebanon, and from the great river, the river Euphrates, even unto the west sea, shall your borders be. 25There shall no man be able to stand before you; the LORD your God shall put the dread of you and the fear of you upon all the land that ye shall tread upon, even as He hath said unto you.

26 27 Behold, I set before you this day the blessing and the curse; the blessing, if ye hearken unto the commandments of the LORD your God, all that I command you this day; 28and the curse, if ye do not hearken unto the commandments of the LORD our God, as many as I command you this day, but turn aside out of the way, to go after other gods, which ye have not known. 29And it shall come to pass, when the LORD thy God hath brought thee in unto the land into which thou goest to inherit, then thou shalt put the blessing upon mount Gerizim, and the curse upon mount Ebal. 30Behold, are they not on the other side Jordan, toward the way where the sun goeth down, in the land of the Canaanites, which dwell in the champaign near Gilgal, beside the lofty oak? 31For ye shall pass over Jordan to go in and inherit the land, which the LORD your God giveth you to inherit always, and ye shall dwell therein. 32And ye shall be careful to observe to do all His statutes, and these judgments, as many as I set before you this day.
