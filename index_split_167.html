Egyptians buried all those that died of them, even all them which the LORD had smitten, every firstborn in the land of Egypt; upon their gods also the LORD executed vengeance. 5And the children of Israel removed from Rameses, and pitched in Succoth; 6and they departed from Succoth, and pitched in Etham, which is part of the wilderness. 7And they removed from Etham, and pitched at the mouth of Hiroth, which is over against Baal-Zephon, and they pitched over against Migdol. 8And they departed from before Hiroth, and passed through the midst of the sea into the wilderness; and they went a three day’s journey through the wilderness and pitched in Marah. 9And they departed from Marah, and came to Elim. And in Elim were twelve fountains of water, and threescore and ten palm trees; and they pitched there by the water. 10And they departed from Elim, and encamped by the Red Sea. 11And they departed from the Red Sea, and encamped in the wilderness of Sin. 12And they took their journey out of the wilderness of Sin, and encamped in Raphaka. 13And they departed from Raphaka, and encamped in Alush. 14And they departed from Alush, and encamped in Rephidim; and there was no water there for the people to drink. 15 16 And they departed from Rephidim, and encamped in the wilderness of Sinai.And they removed from the wilderness of Sinai, and pitched at the Graves of lust. 17And they departed from the Graves of lust, and encamped in Hazeroth. 18And they departed from Hazeroth, and pitched in Rithmah. 19And they departed from Rithmah, and encamped in Rimmon-Perez. 20And they departed from Rimmon-Perez, and pitched in Libnah. 21And they removed from Libnah, and encamped in Rissah. 22And they departed from Rissah, and pitched in Kehelathah. 23And they departed from Kehelathah, and encamped at Shepher. 24And they removed from Shepher, and encamped in Haradah. 25And they went from Haradah, and encamped in Makheloth. 26 27 And they removed from Makheloth, and encamped at Tahath. And they departed from Thath, and pitched at Terah. 28And they removed from Terah, and pitched in Mithkah. 29And they departed from Mithkah, and pitched in Hashmonah. 30And they departed from Hashmonah, and encamped at Moseroth. 31And they departed from Moseroth, and pitched in Bene-jakaan. 32And they removed from Bene-jakaan, and encamped in the mount Gidgad. 33And they departed from the mount Gidgad, and pitched in Jotbathah. 34And they departed from Jotbathah, and encamped in Abronah. 35 36 And they departed from Abronah, and encamped at Ezion-Geber. And they removed from Ezion-Geber, and pitched in the wilderness of Sin. And they departed from the wilderness of Sin and pitched in the wilderness of Paran - this is Kadesh. 37And they departed from Kadesh, and encamped in mount Hor, near the land of Edom. 38And Aaron the priest went up at the commandment of the LORD, and died there in the fortieth year after the departure of the children of Israel out of the land of Egypt, in the fifth month, on the first of the month. 39And Aaron was an hundred and twenty and three years old, when he died in mount Hor. 40And Arad the Canaanitish king (he also dwelt in the land of Canaan), heard of the coming of the children of Israel; 41then they departed from mount Hor and pitched in Zalmonah. 42And they departed from Zalmonah, and pitched in Punon. 43And they departed from Punon, and pitched in Oboth. 44And they departed from Oboth, and pitched in Gai, on the far side of the borders of Moab. 45And they departed from Gai, and pitched in Dibon- Gad. 46And they departed from Dibon-Gad, and encamped in Almon-Diblathaim. 47And they removed from Almon-Diblathaim, and pitched in the mountains of Abarim over against Nebo. 48And they departed from the mountains of Abarim, and encamped to the west of Moab, at Jordan by Jericho. 49And they pitched by Jordan between Jesimoth, even unto Abel-Shittim to the west of Moab.
